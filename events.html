Vocational Events

Presenting at the 2020 Graduate Induction

I was asked to present at the 2020 Graduate Induction with a colleague of mine, Luca Stamatescu. Together we spoke about some of the technological iniatives we had been working on in the Telstra Labs such as AI, ML, Drones and xReality. I also spoke about my graduate journey thus far and the most important things I had learnt.

Presenting at Vantage 2019

I was able to represent Telstra Labs at Vantage 2019. I demonstrated our Humanoid Concierge in partnership with CBA where we promoted the 'Infrastructure-free branch' which looked at future horizon technologies to power a branch via Cloud and Edge Computing.

Presenting at CommsConnect 2019

CommsConnect is a conference that is centred around wireless communication. I was asked to come and present Telstra's over-air streaming potential by demonstrating how we could stream video from a police body camera to a server, run an Object Detection Algorithm and re-stream the video back over 5G.

Presenting at the 2019 Executive Leadership Forum

The Executive Leadership Forum is a Telstra forum where over 200+ executives gather to hear about initiatives in all parts of the business. I was asked to come and present our work that we did with humanoid robots and how we could use them as concierges and tour guides.

Philanthropic Events

Telstra Kids & Monash Toy Library

Telstra Kids Fund is dedicated towards grassroots projects benefitting kids around Australia. Working with Telstra Kids I was able to secure a grant for Monash Toy Libraries, allowing them to reinvest into toys to promote digital literacy for kids. Monash Toy Libraries is a cause that is extremely close to me and it felt great to be able to give back.
