Rotation 2

In my second rotation, I had two different roles as I worked across multiple projects.

The first project I was working with a Telstra Video Analytics product. As project manager, the project was to undertake a full product evaluation and get to the root of the problem. In completing this project, I was able to deliver a 40 page deep-dive technical report on the video analytics software and present to the Product Owners as to why it was not working. This gave the stakeholders an unbiased, in-depth understanding of every bit about the software side of their product, and they were better leveraged to sell their product in the market and also go back to their development team and correct the errors.

The other project was working with IoT Sensor product. This sensor collected information regarding office space optimisation and allowed the end-user to see when was busy, which rooms were busy in an office. Our task was to better utilise this data through Machine Learning. I was able to build a Predictive Algorithm that accurately predicted when desks would be utilised. By building this, we were able to influence office behaviour as if we told people this cluster of desks would be full on Wednesdays by 9:05, people began to come in slightly earlier so that they can sit as a team at our hot desk environments. I was also able to take this Proof of Concept into Production as we then hosted it on Azure and created an API service, allowing people to go to a URL and see the occupancy graph from anywhere.
