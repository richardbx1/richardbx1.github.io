Movie Reviews Written by AI

Telstra Smartspaces Machine Learning Augmentation

The Smartspaces product is a Telstra IoT device that is designed to provide metrics around office space optimisation. I worked with the Product Owner and Software Developers to develop a Machine Learning algorithm that took historic data and then was capable of predicting how full an office space would be and which desks would be free at any given time.

Telstra Video Surveillance Technical Assessment

Telstra Video Surveillance was a Telstra Product that I did a technical assessment on. I worked beetween the Product Owner and Software Developers to run a full dianogstic test on the software and provide reccomendations and improvements on what features were working, how they worked and how they could be further improved for the future.
